About Aurelius University

A legacy of excellence spanning over a century, committed to fostering intellectual growth and societal advancement.

Our Heritage & Mission

Founded in 1892, Aurelius University stands as a beacon of academic excellence and intellectual inquiry. Named after the philosopher-emperor Marcus Aurelius, our institution embodies the principles of wisdom, leadership, and service to humanity.

Located in the heart of Cambridge, Massachusetts, our historic campus spans 200 acres of beautifully landscaped grounds, featuring both classical architecture and state-of-the-art facilities. We are proud to serve over 15,000 students from 80 countries worldwide.

Our mission is to advance knowledge through rigorous scholarship, innovative research, and transformative education that prepares graduates to address the complex challenges of our interconnected world.
